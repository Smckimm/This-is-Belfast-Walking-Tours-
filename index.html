About the Tour

Join Stevie for an unforgettable walking tour of Belfast. From the origins of the River Farset to the Albert Clock, Custom House Square, the Cathedral Quarter, and City Hall— explore the history, politics, and culture that shaped our city. Hear the stories of Henry Joy McCracken, Thomas McCabe, and the ordinary people who lived through extraordinary times.
