About the Tour

My name is Stevie, born, bred and proud to hail from the historic and unique city of Belfast.

I have a passion for history, culture and notable people all of which Belfast has in abundance. Having grown up with the troubles - being born in 1969 - I have the lived experience of what our city went through and how it has risen to rightly be one of the premium city breaks in the world!
