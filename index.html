Born, Raised & Made in Belfast

I have a passion for the history, culture, and notable people — all of which Belfast and Northern Ireland have in abundance. Having grown up during the Troubles, being born in 1969, I have the lived experience of what our city went through and how it has risen to rightly be one of the premium city breaks in the world.

My tour captures the origins of the city from the humble banks of the hidden River Farset through to rebellion, industry, conflict, and peace. Come and walk with me to hear the real story of Belfast.
